VERNA

The Hyundai Verna is a stylish and sophisticated compact sedan known for its elegant design, advanced technology, and impressive performance. Launched in 2006, the Verna has evolved through multiple generations, offering a refined driving experience with a choice of efficient petrol and diesel engines. It features a sleek exterior with a bold front grille and modern LED lighting, while the interior boasts a premium feel with a spacious cabin, advanced infotainment system, and numerous comfort features. The Verna is equipped with comprehensive safety measures, making it a reliable and attractive option for both urban and long-distance driving. The Hyundai Verna combines modern aesthetics, a comfortable and feature-rich interior, and robust performance, making it a strong competitor in the compact sedan segment.

Engine Options:

Petrol Engines:

  • 1.5-liter MPi: Produces around 115 PS (113 hp) and 144 Nm of torque.
  • 1.0-liter Turbo GDi: Produces around 120 PS (118 hp) and 172 Nm of torque

Diesel Engine:

  • 1.5-liter CRDi: Produces around 115 PS (113 hp) and 250 Nm of torque.

Transmission:

  • 6-speed manual
  • 6-speed automatic
  • 7-speed Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) for the 1.0-liter Turbo GDi
  • Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) for the 1.5-liter MPi
