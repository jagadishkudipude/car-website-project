CRETA

The Hyundai Creta is a popular compact SUV known for its dynamic design, spacious interior, and versatile performance. Since its launch in 2015, the Creta has garnered a strong following due to its robust build quality and array of advanced features. It offers a choice of efficient petrol and diesel engines, catering to various driving preferences. The interior is well-appointed with modern amenities like a touchscreen infotainment system, premium sound options, and comprehensive safety features. The Creta's elevated driving position and smooth handling make it ideal for both urban commuting and off-road adventures, solidifying its position as a favored choice in the compact SUV market. The Hyundai Creta combines a robust engine lineup, a host of modern amenities, and a comprehensive suite of safety features, making it a versatile and appealing choice in the compact SUV market.

Engine Options:

Petrol Engines:

  • 1.5-liter MPi: Produces around 115 PS (113 hp) and 144 Nm of torque.
  • 1.4-liter Turbo GDi: Produces around 140 PS (138 hp) and 242 Nm of torque.

Diesel Engine:

  • 1.5-liter CRDi: Produces around 115 PS (113 hp) and 250 Nm of torque.

Transmission:

  • 6-speed manual
  • 6-speed automatic
  • 7-speed Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT) for the 1.4-liter Turbo GDi
  • Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) for the 1.5-liter MPi

Dimensions:

  • Length: Approximately 4,300 mm
  • Width: Approximately 1,790 mm
  • Height: Approximately 1,635 mm

Fuel Efficiency:

  • Varies by engine and transmission, generally ranging between 17 to 21 km/l (combined).

Interior and Features:

  • 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Digital instrument cluster
  • Bose premium sound system (in higher trims)
  • Automatic climate control
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Ventilated front seats
  • Wireless charging
  • >Rear AC vents

Safety:

  • Multiple airbags (front, side, and curtain)
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD)
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • Hill Start Assist Control (HAC)
  • Vehicle Stability Management (VSM)
  • Rearview camera with parking sensors
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)
  • ISOFIX child seat
