GRAND VITARA

The Suzuki Grand Vitara is a robust compact SUV that blends rugged off-road capability with refined urban comfort. First introduced in 1988, the Grand Vitara has evolved through several generations, offering a versatile driving experience with its powerful engines and advanced 4x4 systems. Its design features a bold exterior with a prominent front grille and sleek lines, while the interior provides ample space, modern infotainment options, and comfortable seating. Equipped with a range of safety features and practical amenities, the Grand Vitara is well-suited for both adventurous terrains and everyday city driving, making it a popular choice for drivers seeking a reliable and versatile SUV. The Suzuki Grand Vitara combines rugged performance with modern conveniences and safety features, making it a versatile choice for both off-road adventures and urban commuting.

Engine Options:

Petrol Engines:

  • 1.5-liter K15C DualJet: Produces around 103 PS (102 hp) and 136 Nm of torque.
  • 1.4-liter BoosterJet Turbo (mild hybrid): Produces around 129 PS (127 hp) and 235 Nm of torque (available in some markets).

Hybrid Engine:

  • 1.5-liter petrol hybrid: Combines a petrol engine with an electric motor to produce around 116 PS (114 hp) and 141 Nm of torque.

Transmission:

  • 5-speed manual
  • 6-speed automatic
  • Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Dimensions:

  • Length: Approximately 4,345 mm
  • Width: Approximately 1,775 mm
  • Height: Approximately 1,645 mm
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 2,600 mm

Fuel Efficiency:

  • Varies by engine and transmission, generally ranging between 18 to 23 km/l (combined).

Interior and Features:

  • 9-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Multi-function steering wheel
  • >Automatic climate control
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Wireless charging
  • Rear AC vents

Safety:

  • Multiple airbags (front, side, and curtain)
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD)
  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP)
  • Hill Hold Control

  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)
  • ISOFIX child seat mounts
