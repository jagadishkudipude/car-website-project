SWIFT

The Suzuki Swift is a compact hatchback celebrated for its sporty design, agile handling, and efficient performance. Since its introduction in 2000, the Swift has gained a reputation for being a fun-to-drive car with a stylish exterior and a well-crafted interior. It offers a range of efficient petrol engines, making it ideal for city driving and daily commutes. The interior features modern amenities like a touchscreen infotainment system, comfortable seating, and advanced safety measures. The Swift's compact size, coupled with its responsive handling and fuel efficiency, makes it a popular choice for drivers seeking a practical yet dynamic driving experience. The Suzuki Swift offers a blend of sporty aesthetics, practicality, and efficiency, making it a popular choice among compact hatchbacks.

Engine Options:

Petrol Engines:

  • 1.2-liter DualJet VVT: Produces around 83 PS (82 hp) and 113 Nm of torque.
  • 1.0-liter BoosterJet Turbo: Produces around 102 PS (101 hp) and 160 Nm of torque (available in some markets).

Hybrid Engine:

  • 1.2-liter DualJet SHVS: Mild hybrid system producing around 90 PS (89 hp) and 113 Nm of torque (available in some markets).

Transmission:

  • 5-speed manual
  • 5-speed automated manual transmission (AMT)
  • Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Dimensions:

Fuel Efficiency:

  • Varies by engine and transmission, generally ranging between 20 to 24 km/l (combined).
