I20

The Hyundai i20 is a popular subcompact car known for its stylish design, advanced features, and reliable performance . First introduced in 2008, the i20 has undergone several upgrades, offering a blend of comfort and technology with efficient engines and modern safety features. Its sleek exterior, spacious interior, and user-friendly infotainment system make it a favored choice for urban driving and daily commutes. The i20 competes well in the global market, appealing to a wide range of customers seeking a balanced mix of affordability and quality. The Hyundai i20 blends performance, efficiency, and a host of modern features, making it a strong contender in the subcompact car market.

Engine Options:

Petrol Engines:

  • 1.2-liter Kappa Dual VTVT: Produces around 83 PS (82 hp) and 114 Nm of torque.
  • 1.0-liter Turbo GDi: Produces around 120 PS (118 hp) and 172 Nm of torque.

Diesel Engine:

  • 1.5-liter U2 CRDi: Produces around 100 PS (99 hp) and 240 Nm of torque.

Transmission:

  • 5-speed manual
  • 6-speed manual
  • 6-speed Intelligent Manual Transmission (iMT)
  • 7-speed Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT)
  • Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Dimensions:

  • Length: Approximately 3,995 mm
  • Width: Approximately 1,775 mm
  • Height: Approximately 1,505 mm
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 2,580 mm

Fuel Efficiency:

  • Varies by engine and transmission, generally ranging between 18 to 25 km/l (combined).

Interior and Features:

  • Touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Digital instrument cluster
  • Automatic climate control
  • Wireless charging
  • Bose premium sound system (in higher trims)
  • Rear AC vents

Safety:

  • Multiple airbags (front, side, and curtain)
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD)
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • Hill Assist Control (HAC)
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)
  • ISOFIX child seat mounts
